# Homepage Copy Generation Prompt

## Purpose
Generate compelling homepage copy with a clear value proposition for local businesses.

---

## Prompt Template

**For [Business Type]: [Business Name]**

You are an expert website copywriter specializing in local businesses. Generate homepage copy for a [business type] that converts visitors into customers.

### Business Context:
- **Business Type:** [e.g., Salon, Cafe, Clinic, Agency]
- **Business Name:** [Name]
- **Target Audience:** [Description of ideal customers]
- **Unique Selling Point:** [What makes them different]
- **Location:** [City/Area]

### Required Sections:

1. **Hero Headline** (8-12 words)
   - Should immediately convey the main benefit
   - Use power words and emotional triggers
   - Make the value proposition crystal clear

2. **Subheadline** (15-25 words)
   - Explain what makes this business different
   - Build on the hero headline

3. **Hero Copy** (50-75 words)
   - Describe the transformation/benefit
   - Address pain points
   - Use conversational tone

4. **Social Proof Section** (2-3 bullet points)
   - Include credibility markers
   - Years in business, customer count, or awards

5. **Call-to-Action Button Text**
   - Primary CTA (e.g., "Book Your Appointment")
   - Secondary CTA (e.g., "Learn More")

### Tone Guidelines:
- Keep language simple and conversational
- Avoid jargon
- Be persuasive but authentic
- Focus on benefits, not features

### Output Format:
```
# [Business Name] - Homepage Copy

## Hero Section
**Headline:** [Your headline]
**Subheadline:** [Your subheadline]

## Hero Copy
[Your copy]

## Social Proof
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

## CTAs
- Primary: [CTA text]
- Secondary: [CTA text]
```

---

## Example Input
**Business Type:** Salon | **Business Name:** Glow Beauty Studio | **Target Audience:** Women 25-45 seeking premium hair services | **USP:** Expert stylists + natural products | **Location:** Downtown Manhattan

## Example Output
```
# Glow Beauty Studio - Homepage Copy

## Hero Section
**Headline:** Transform Your Look, Transform Your Confidence
**Subheadline:** Expert stylists using premium natural products for stunning results

## Hero Copy
Every head of hair tells a story. At Glow Beauty Studio, we don't just cut and color—we create transformations. Our expert stylists combine precision technique with premium natural products to give you hair that looks amazing and feels even better. Whether it's a fresh cut, vibrant color, or complete makeover, we'll make you feel like the best version of yourself.

## Social Proof
- 8+ years serving Downtown Manhattan
- 2,000+ happy customers
- Certified in color correction & advanced styling

## CTAs
- Primary: Book Your Transformation
- Secondary: View Our Gallery
```
